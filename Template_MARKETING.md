# Marketing & Growth Strategy

## 1. Core Messaging
- "Meet your baby for the price of one coffee (5,900 KRW)."
- Focus on "Extreme Realism" to differentiate from "Cartoon-like" competitors.

## 2. Channels
- **Instagram**: Sponsored posts featuring Before/After comparisons.
- **Mom-cafes**: Targeted threads on "Meeting the baby before birth".

## 3. Growth Loop
- Easy SNS sharing on the Result page.
- **Urgency & Trust**: Provide certainty of high quality rather than monetary refunds (Align with Legal).
